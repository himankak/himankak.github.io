Himanka is a Ph.D. researcher with Roma Tre University, Rome, Italy. Himanka received his Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Assam Don Bosco University (ADBU), Guwahati, India and Master of Technology (M. Tech) degree in Information Technology from North Eastern Regional Institute of Science and Technology (NERIST), Itanagar, India. During his Ph.D. he was an Early Stage Researcher (ESR) for the project ENCASE, a H2020 Marie Curie RISE funded project and has been associated as an ESR with CyRIC and Signal Generix, which are located in cities Nicosia and Limassol respectively, both located in Cyprus. He was also an ESR with Telefonica I + D, Barcelona, Spain. He also spent a research duration as an Erasmus+ UI/UX researcher intern for a startup named TimeVillage, which is based in Stockholm, Sweden. Earlier to that he had worked as a project staff of Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Guwahati, India in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering for the research and development project Integration and Maintenance of Virtual Lab, with the task of lab experiments simulation development.

Technical Interests

Currently I am invested in the research of state-of-the-art adversarial machine learning and deep learning models for computer vision, mobile user recognition through behavioural biometrics, and natural language processing. Study and improvement of robustness of semantic segmentation, biometric verification and identification models' and systems' performance, is also included in my research. Quality mobile behavioural biometric research and synthetic image datasets are rare and I am contributing my solutions to this open research area. Broad technical areas of interests are mentioned below.

  • Adversarial machine learning
  • Research on behavioural biometrics
  • Programming languages
  • Android apps for user behaviour study
  • Behavioural data research
  • Web component development

Projects

During these past few years I have had the privilege to contribute as a researcher in these two prestigious projects mentioned below. My contributions to these projects are discussed in my CV. My personal Ph.D. projects are maintained in my private GitHub repositories.

ENCASE, funded by H2020 Marie Curie Fellowship

ENCASE (ENhancing seCurity and privAcy in the Social wEb): a user-centered approach for the protection of minors aims at leveraging the latest advances in usable security and privacy to design and implement a browser-based architecture for the protection of minors from malicious actors in online social networks (OSNs).
