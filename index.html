I am a PhD researcher with Biometric Systems and Multimedia Forensics Lab of Roma Tre University, Rome, Italy, under the supervision of Prof. Patrizio Campisi from 2017. I received my Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Assam Don Bosco University (ADBU), Guwahati, India in 2013 and Master of Technology (M. Tech) degree in Information Technology from North Eastern Regional Institute of Science and Technology (NERIST), Itanagar, India in 2015.

Technical Interests

I am currently invested in the research of mobile user recognition through behavioural biometrics, which comprises of traits such as touch dynamics, swipes, gait, signature, etc. Study and improvement of verification and identification system performance, based on state-of-the-art machine and deep learning models, which considers human behaviour on mobile devices is included in my research. Quality mobile behavioural biometric research datasests are rare and I am contributing my solutions in this open research area. Broad technical areas of interests are mentioned below.

  • Research on behavioural biometrics
  • Coding with various languages
  • Papers on Biometrics, HCI, etc.
  • Machine and deep learning
  • Android apps for user behaviour study
  • Behavioural data research

ENCASE, funded by H2020 Marie Curie Fellowship

ENCASE (ENhancing seCurity and privAcy in the Social wEb): a user-centered approach for the protection of minors) aims at leveraging the latest advances in usable security and privacy to design and implement a browser-based architecture for the protection of minors from malicious actors in online social networks (OSNs).
